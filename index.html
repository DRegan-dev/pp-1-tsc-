Why join Liminexus?

Sightseeing

Why limit yourself to mere planetary vacations when you can jump through time and space? Whether it's a quick coffee in Victorian England, a weekend at the height of the Roman Empire, or a gala in a parallel universe where dinosaurs rule, our club offers the ultimate in exclusive getaways.

Abduction Skills

Ever found yourself accidentally abducting a 20th-century human and then having to explain it at the next galactic council meeting? Join our club and you'll have a ready-made alibi! "Oh, we were just part of a cultural exchange program," sounds much better than, "Oops, wrong planet."

Epic Social Events

From interdimensional raves that transcend space and time to themed parties involving friendly human abductions, our gatherings are the stuff of legend. Dance with beings from parallel universes, exchange stories with time-traveling adventurers, and make memories that span across the multiverse.
